About

Anderson "Niko" Pereira Silva

I'm a researcher in cognitive systems and human behavior, with a background in forensic investigation, cryptography, and decentralized architecture. I've been building things since 2006 — not because someone asked me to, but because I can't stop thinking of new ones.

My work lives at the edges: audio-only RPG engines, P2P protocols, privacy tools, anime projects, order management systems running on Android. I'm equally interested in how systems behave and how people do.

Outside the screen I hold a 6th Dan in Bujutsu and Goshinjutsu, cook my own recipes, and listen to a lot of Industrial Metal. Based in São Paulo's Zona Leste.
